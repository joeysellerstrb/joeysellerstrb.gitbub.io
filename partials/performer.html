Performance and Ensemble Information

Sellers has led his 11 piece ensemble, the "Jazz Aggregation" starting at age 18. They have garnered critical praise on four recordings.

Joey Sellers Trio is Sellers’ latest endeavor, closing boundaries between improvisation and composition.

Joey Sellers Solo Trombone – Sellers’ Solo Trombone CD, "What The. . .?"  received four stars in Downbeat. No one has any idea how to categorize it. He performs solo trombone under special circumstances. . .

Sellers has been a guest artist with many ensembles, including Eastman School of Music’s Lab Ensemble, several High Schools, several Community Bands, and god knows what else.
